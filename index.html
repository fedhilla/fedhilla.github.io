Risk Management

IMPLEMENTASI METODE DELTA-GAMMA DALAM PERHITUNGAN VALUE AT RISK (VAR) UNTUK PORTOFOLIO OBLIGASI PEMERINTAH BERBASIS DURASI MACULAY DAN KONVEKSITAS

📄 Artikel

This paper applies the Delta-Gamma method to calculate Value at Risk (VaR) for government bond portfolios, incorporating Macaulay duration and convexity for enhanced accuracy. Using FR0100 and FR0101 bond data (December 2023-December 2024), the study demonstrates that this approach provides robust risk evaluation and aids in optimizing investment decisions.

Risk Management of SMGR Stock Using Value-at-Risk Methods

💻 Repository

This project analyzes SMGR stock risk using four Value-at-Risk (VaR) methods Cornish-Fisher, Historical Simulation, Variance-Covariance, and Monte Carlo. It compares downside risk under different assumptions, offering insights into method reliability and suitability for SMGR’s volatility.
